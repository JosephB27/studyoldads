# Curation Taste

Current north star:

- The first twelve seed-board ads are the bar.
- Canon copy-led ads earn priority: Apple, Porsche, Volkswagen, Rolex, Rolls-Royce, Volvo, BMW, Daihatsu, Audemars Piguet.
- London Underground works when the poster is graphically sharp, not merely historical.
- Current London favorites: `Fly the Tube`, `The Lure of the Underground`, `Keep Warm`, `Take Cover`.
- `Coca-Cola 5 Cents` stays because it has color, brand force, and immediate grid presence.
- Old black-and-white newspaper or catalog-style ads should be cut from the wall unless the idea is exceptional.

## Earns a Spot

A candidate should feel like one of these:

- a brutal line with a lot of white space;
- luxury/status copy that is smarter than the category;
- automotive self-awareness with a real strategic turn;
- technology made obvious in one sentence;
- transit/poster design with clean graphic force.

Reject candidates that feel like:

- archival filler;
- dense newspaper copy;
- generic vintage packaging;
- historically interesting but visually dead;
- black-and-white scans without a sharp idea.
